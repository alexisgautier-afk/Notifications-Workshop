Workshop Agenda · Slide 1 of 4
Today's Objectives
& Stakes
  • B-Style is the Sales Associates' daily tool. Notifications are the first touchpoint that brings them back into the app. They set the tone for engagement.
  • Since SAs have no existing notification system, this is a greenfield design: we set the standard from day one.
  • What we decide today: which triggers to activate, what SAs read, which channel, and where they land after tapping.
  • Risk of getting it wrong: ignored notifications = missed client moments. Too many = SA distrust of the app.
Decision Goal
Leave this session with validated answers on 4 questions for each notification type: trigger, channel, copy, and destination.
Risk to Mitigate
Ignored notifications = missed client moments.
Over-notification = SA distrust of the app.
4 Decisions Per Notification
1Trigger: what fires the notification
2Channel: push, in-app, or both
3Copy: exact title and body text
4Destination: screen after tap

Section 2 · Channel Strategy
📲
Push Notification
Sent to the device lock screen or tray. Interrupts the SA. Best for time-sensitive moments. Dismissed once seen. Won't persist in the app.
🔔
In-App Notification Center
Visible when the SA opens B-Style. Passive, non-interruptive. Persists until read or dismissed. Better for lower-urgency updates.
Notification Push In-App Center Rationale to Discuss
Appointment Reminder TBD TBD Time-sensitive. Push is likely needed. Does it also persist in center?
Appointment Assigned TBD TBD Real-time awareness vs. lower urgency. Push may be optional.
New Client Assigned TBD TBD Informational. In-app center may be sufficient alone.
New Campaign Available TBD TBD Broadcast. Center likely sufficient. Push may feel intrusive.
Dual channel: Can some notifications use both: push for immediacy + persists in center?
Dismissal: Should SAs be able to dismiss entries from the notification center, or do some persist until acted on?
Unread cap: Is there a maximum number of unread notifications displayed in the center?

Section 3 · Notification Copy · Working Examples to React To
Notification Push Title ~30 chars Push Body ~80 chars In-App Center Text richer, persists
Appointment Reminder "Appointment in 2 hours" "Sophie Martin, Personal Shopping, 3:00 PM" "Reminder: Your appointment with Sophie Martin is at 3:00 PM today. Personal Shopping."
Appointment Assigned "New appointment for you" "Sophie Martin, Tomorrow at 11:00 AM" "An appointment with Sophie Martin has been assigned to you for tomorrow at 11:00 AM."
New Client Assigned "New client in your portfolio" "Sophie Martin has been assigned to you" "Sophie Martin has been added to your client portfolio. Tap to view her profile."
New Campaign Available "New campaign available" "Summer Collection is live. Tap to discover." "The Summer Collection campaign is now live. Tap to view assets and client targeting."
Privacy question
Should the client's name appear? Personalisation vs. privacy on a shared or visible device.
Language
Does copy need to work in multiple languages? Who owns translation and maintenance?
Push constraints
Title ~30 chars, body ~80 chars. No context assumed. SA may be off-shift or off-device.
In-app richness
More client detail, timestamp, read/unread label. Can include action buttons.
